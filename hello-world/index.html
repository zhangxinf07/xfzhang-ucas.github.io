Welcome

Xinfeng Zhang is a tenured assistant professor and doctoral supervisor of the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. His research areas include video encoding and decoding technology, quality evaluation and video enhancement processing, point cloud compression and processing, etc. He has authored and coauthored more than 170 high-level academic papers in important international journals and conferences, and won awards such as the IEEE Video Coding Standard Contribution Award, 5 Best Paper Awards in International/Domestic Best Journals (Conferences), and the Weiqiao National Science and Technology President's Teaching Fellowship.He has made more than 20 domestic and international coding standard technical proposals and 11 domestic authorized patents. He is also a senior member of IEEE, serving as an editorial board member of three international journals in this field and a program committee member of multiple domestic and foreign academic conferences. He presided over more than ten projects/topics including the National Natural Science Fund Project for Outstanding Young Scholars (overseas), general projects, joint fund (key support) projects, key research and development projects of the Ministry of Science and Technology, and the "Hundred Talents Program" of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Internship and Graduate Students (both Ph.D. and Master) Opening : I am looking for self-motivated candidates who have solid mathematical backgrounds, strong English ability, and strong coding skills. If you are interested in doing research on video/image coding, processing and quality assessment, point cloud compression and processing, please send detailed CV to me.
